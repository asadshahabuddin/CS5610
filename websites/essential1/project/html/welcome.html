Overview

Console is the center of your world. It gives you the ability to do most of your daily activities using a single application. News, Weather Information, Content Search and Social Discovery are part of the suite. Sift through my offerings as a guest or have it full-featured as a registered user. More services may be added in time as I expect my application to replace many others.

The idea manifested from the concept of a central repository for all data related services. My ambition has always been to keep you on this page based on its completeness and the breadth of useful features. You shouldn't have to deal with a plethora of tabs and applications to keep track of a multitude of relevant information. The simplest way to eliminate such a scenario would be to provide that information here. After you create a Console account and log in to its services, you can keep track of pertinent information and your daily activities using my catalogue of services. You get a personalized environment with things that you care about, helping you stay organized and informed.

The application is free and always will be. Give it a chance; you won't regret it.
