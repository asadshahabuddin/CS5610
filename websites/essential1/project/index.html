Overview

Console is the center of your world. It gives you the ability to do most of your daily activities using a single application. News, Weather Information, Content Search and Expense Tracking are part of the suite. User logging and session tracking are under construction and should be available in the near future. More services may be added in time as we expect our application to replace many others.

The idea manifested from the concept of a central repository for all data related services. My ambition has always been to keep you on this page based on its completeness and the breadth of useful features. I don't want you to have to deal with a plethora of tabs and applications to keep track of a multitude of relevant information. The simplest way to eliminate such a scenario would be provide that information here.
